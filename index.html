Gather your notes in one place with the new
iOS Notes Copy-Paste Function!

When starting a new note in the app, open the camera and take a picture of your notes.
If you're satisfied with the image, select "Use Photo", then "Copy Text" and watch as your notes appear in the app!

Your notes are now digitalized and ready to be edited and/or saved - amazing, right?
